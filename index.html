About Me

Ever since I was young, I loved journaling. I had diaries with a lock and key, composition notebooks for journaling in school, and sketchbooks for ideas and doodles. As time went on the way I’ve used journals has evolved. I discovered a new way to journal, and I love it. It is called : bullet journaling, AKA BuJo!

What Is A Bullet Journal?

Bullet Journaling is a great hobby and can boost your productivity! It was developed by Ryder Carroll and first shown to the public on Youtube in 2013. A bullet journal is a planner, a diary, a sketchbook! It can help you stay on track with work and school, and it can be a great creative outlet.

Bullet Journal Types

One of the reasons for the bullet journal’s popularity is because of how customizable it is. There are many types of bullet journals out there. It can be minimalist, organized, messy, artsy, etc. It can also be digital, by using note taking apps like Evernote, for example.

Bullet Journal Materials

All you need in order to make a bullet journal is a pen / pencil and paper. A classic bullet journal would use: A dot grid journal, a ruler, pens, highlighters, and markers.

Bullet Journal Community

After the first video in 2013, bullet journaling has become widely popular. You can share your bullet journal on sites like Instagram, Pinterest, Reddit, almost any social media site. The community is encouraging and helpful when it comes to tips, tricks, and support.
